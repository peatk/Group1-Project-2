Public Libraries VS Classroom Libraries

What This Means

Libraries

The percentage of bans that were implemented into public libraries.

Classrooms

The percentage of bans that were implemented into classroom libraries and schools.

Libaries and Classrooms

The percentage of bans that were implemented into both public libraries and schools.

Pending Investigation

The percentage of bans that are still undergoing a review process.

Ongoing Bans

A significant portion of bans are currently under pending investigation status in some of the states like Tennessee which has around 91%. Texas which has around 67%, and Florida which has around 65%.

Impact

It may imply stricter policies regarding book releases to the public and a more controlled approach to educational materials.
